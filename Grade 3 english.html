Grade 3 English Quiz

There are 8 questions

All the best!!!!

One day, Ann and Frank went to the lake with their dog Rover. Rover can swim well, so Frank threw a stick into the water for him to fetch.

1. What did Rover fetch in the water?

2. Why couldn’t Rover catch the rabbit?

3. What might have happened if Ann and Frank stayed later?

4. How do you think Rover felt about not catching the rabbit?

Six blind men stood by the roadside every day and asked for money from people who passed. They had heard of elephants, but they had never seen one because they were blind.

5. Why were the blind men on the roadside?

6. What does the elephant’s ear feel like to one of the men?

7. How do the blind men treat one another?

8. What does this passage try to teach us?
